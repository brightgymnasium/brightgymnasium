Our Facility

The facility is planned across three floors so every workout has a dedicated space. This gives members more comfort, better movement and a focused training experience.

Cardio and free workout area for warmups, stamina, fat loss, functional movements and daily fitness routines.

Second Floor

Strength training floor with equipment for muscle gain, progressive training, body shaping and power workouts.

Third Floor

Dedicated yoga space for flexibility, breathing, relaxation, balance, mobility and overall wellness.
